A note on methodology

Methodology

We hold a single position about money software: the tool that asks for your bank password is the tool you will eventually regret installing. Forecasting is arithmetic, not surveillance. Four honest inputs and a curve drawn in front of you tell a clearer story than a thousand reconciled transactions told by someone you cannot meet.

The figures shown on this site are computed by a small, documented set of equations. We publish them. You can read them. If a number on your screen ever surprises you, the math is there to inspect.

A boutique forecasting house. Based in Europe. Independent.
