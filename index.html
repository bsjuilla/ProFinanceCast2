Questions, answered

Most asked.

Do I have to connect my bank?

Never. ProFinanceCast doesn't read or store transaction data. You type four numbers, the forecast happens in your browser, and the data stays on your device.

How is the forecast calculated?

A transparent compounding model: monthly savings grow at a configurable rate, debt amortizes against your monthly payment, and inflation is pulled from FRED CPI data. The full formula is on GitHub.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. One click in settings. No retention emails, no friction. There's also a 30-day refund on the first Pro charge, no questions asked.

Does it work outside the US?

It works in eighty-plus countries with localised take-home pay calculations and currency. Forecasts and goals are currency-agnostic.

What if I'm not great with money?

That's exactly who ProFinanceCast is for. It's calm by design. No streaks, no nudges, no shame loops. Just a clear picture of where you are and where you're going.

Who builds this?

A small, independent team of designers and engineers who got tired of finance apps that felt like trading platforms. We answer support emails personally, usually the same day.
