Questions, answered plainly.

Do you connect to my bank?

No. We never ask for bank credentials. You type four numbers. The forecast runs in your browser.

How accurate is the forecast?

The math is exact. The future is not. We assume a 6% real return on invested savings and a 3% wage inflation by default. You can change every assumption.

What happens if I cancel Pro?

Your account drops back to Free. Your data stays. Your last twelve months of saved scenarios remain readable.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell, share, or advertise against your data. We make money from subscriptions.

Is this investment advice?

No. ProFinanceCast is a forecasting tool. We do not recommend specific securities. For investment advice, speak to a regulated advisor.

What is the Founders tier?

A limited one-time purchase for the first 500 households. You get lifetime Pro access, a founders badge, and quarterly office hours with the team.
