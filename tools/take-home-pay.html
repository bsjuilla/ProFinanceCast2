What is included.

US: federal income tax against the 2026 projected brackets, state income tax for the five supported states, plus FICA (Social Security at 6.2% up to the $176,100 wage base, Medicare at 1.45%, plus the 0.9% additional-Medicare threshold). The standard deduction is applied automatically. UK: income tax against the rUK 2026 bands with personal-allowance taper above £100k, and employee Class 1 NI at 8% / 2%.

What is left out.

Pre-tax 401(k) and HSA contributions, employer benefits, local city taxes, and Scotland's separate income-tax bands. These can shift take-home by a few percentage points; for a precise figure, see the methodology.

Brackets are 2026 projected. Estimates for planning, not tax advice.
