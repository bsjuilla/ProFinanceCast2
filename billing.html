Questions

Things people ask before signing up.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro runs month-to-month or annually; cancellation is one click in your account and the subscription stops at the end of the current period. No phone call, no save offer, no retention flow. Your data remains accessible until the period ends, and exportable forever.

What is the refund policy?

Fourteen days, no questions. Email us and we return the charge in full. Applies to Pro monthly, Pro annual, and Founders. The number on the invoice is the number that goes back; we do not pro-rate or deduct a processing fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and SEPA direct debit for European customers. Payments are processed by Stripe; we never see the card number. Receipts arrive by email immediately and the full invoice history is downloadable from your account.

Is there a free trial?

No trial, because there is a Free tier. Use the free forecast for as long as you like; upgrade when the Pro features become worth nine dollars a month. We would rather you take a year than be on a clock.

Can I upgrade or change plans later?

Yes. Move from Free to Pro at any time. Move from Pro monthly to Pro annual and we pro-rate the change. Move from Pro to Founders and we credit the unused portion of your subscription against the one-time price.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your forecast lives in your browser; we do not hold a server-side copy. Export to CSV or JSON before you go and you keep everything. Re-importing later is a single click and your account is reactivated where you left it.
