What this assumes

Three inputs, plainly stated.

Methodology →

The twelve-month cash forecast is built from your recurring transactions plus three modest assumptions.

  1. Inflation on expenses. Expense categories rise 2.5% annualised; the bars above already include a mild seasonal pattern (utilities tick up in winter, groceries dip in summer).
  2. Flat income. Salary and freelance income hold steady through the window; raises and new contracts are not modelled here. Use scenarios to test them.
  3. No one-off events. Bonuses, tax refunds, large purchases, and emergency outlays are excluded. The forecast describes the household's underlying rhythm, not its surface weather.

Full methodology, including how the seasonal pattern is generated and where rounding occurs, is documented on the about page.
