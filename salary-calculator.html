How this works

Four lines, plain language.

Methodology →
  1. Federal income tax. Bracketed against your filing status, applied after the standard deduction and any pre-tax contributions you enter.
  2. State income tax. California, New York, Texas, Florida, and Washington are supported; the latter three levy no wage income tax.
  3. FICA. Social Security at 6.2% to the wage base, Medicare at 1.45% on every dollar, plus a 0.9% additional Medicare surtax above $200,000 single / $250,000 joint.
  4. Pre-tax deductions. 401(k), HSA, and FSA reduce federal taxable income; California still taxes 401(k) and HSA contributions but FSA and SS/Medicare follow federal treatment.
