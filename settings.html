Section four

Connected services.

There are no integrations to manage, by design.

Nothing connected. Nothing to disconnect.

ProFinanceCast does not connect to bank accounts. Numbers are yours to enter and yours to keep. The household model lives in your browser; exports come from this page. We do not call Plaid, Yodlee, Truelayer, or any bank API. We never ask for online-banking credentials, and there is no integration marketplace planned.

Section five

Danger zone.

Two irreversible actions, both gated by an explicit confirmation.

Export all data

A JSON bundle of the household model: figures, debts, goals, recurring transactions, and the full ledger. Open in any text editor.

Delete account

Removes the household model and all preferences. Cannot be undone. Export first if you want a copy.

Irreversible

Delete this account?

This removes the household model, preferences, and the full ledger. Demo accounts hold nothing persistent, so the action is a UI demonstration here — no data leaves the browser.
