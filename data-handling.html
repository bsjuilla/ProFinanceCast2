Four rules

The four rules of the figures you type.

Every number that appears in the forecast originates with you. The rules below are operational, not marketing claims; the production code is structured to keep them true.

  • Figures are entered by you. Income, expenses, debt balances, savings — you type them. We do not import them from a bank, an aggregator, or any third party. There is no integration to opt out of because there is no integration to begin with.
  • Stored locally to your session. The numbers are held in your browser’s local storage on the device you are using. If you sign out, switch devices, or clear browser data, the figures clear with it. We do not warehouse a server-side copy.
  • Not transmitted. The forecast is computed in the browser; the figures do not leave it. The only data that travels to our servers is the metadata required to operate a subscription (email, plan, payment token), and that data does not include the financial figures.
  • Never shared. We do not sell, syndicate, lease, profile, or enrich the figures you enter, with any party, for any reason. There is no analytics processor, no advertising vendor, and no customer-data-platform sitting behind this site.
