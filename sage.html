How Sage reads your numbers.

Methodology →

Sage takes the same monthly figures the dashboard shows — income, expenses, balances, rates — and walks them forward under the assumptions you’ve set. Nothing is improvised; every claim ties back to a number you can trace.

Responses are written in three movements: a one-paragraph answer, two or three observations worth keeping, and a single closing line. That shape is deliberate. It mirrors how a human advisor would brief you over coffee.
