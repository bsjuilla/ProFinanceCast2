A short note

The first release is on the bench.

The public version of ProFinanceCast is scheduled for roughly the third quarter of 2026. Until then this page is a placeholder; there is nothing to ship yet, and we’d rather say so plainly than fabricate version numbers.

When the first release lands, every entry on this page will be dated, written in plain prose, and linked to the methodology change that produced it. Cosmetic edits are not given a version number; only changes to the forecast model, the tools, or the pricing earn one.

In the meantime, the journal carries the longer-form notes on what we are working on and why.
