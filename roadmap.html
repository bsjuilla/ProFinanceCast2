Shape

Forecast first. Nothing else.

The roadmap is governed by a small set of commitments. ProFinanceCast is a forecasting tool; the work that ships first is the work that sharpens the forecast. There will be no bank connections, no aggregator integrations, no third-party data brokers stitched into the product, ever.

Within those bounds the upcoming bench is a scenario-stacking view, a property-and-mortgage module, additional country tax tables, and a longer-horizon retirement projection. We list a roadmap so visitors can see what we have committed to, what we have deferred, and the reasoning on each item.

The position is documented at /about. When the public roadmap is published it will sit on this page, with dates, rationale, and the explicit list of what we are choosing not to build.
